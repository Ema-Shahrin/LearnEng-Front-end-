Creative Writing Practice

Test your creative writing skills with these engaging activities.

1. Describe the scene in this image and importance of it. Be creative!
2. Rearrange the words to form a correct sentence: "running cats park the are in"
3. Write a short story (3-4 sentences) based on the following prompt: "Once upon a time there was an old wise man."
4. Continue the story: "The forest was eerily quiet, until..."
5. Transform the following statement into a question: "They were walking home."
6. Describe your dream vacation using at least three different adjectives.
7. Use the following words in a sentence: "fantastic", "adventure", "unknown"
8. Write a creative description of a mysterious place you've never been.
9. Imagine you are a time traveler. Write a short journal entry from the future.
10. Write a poem about the changing seasons (3-4 lines).
